about us

We are Kingdom Planters a non-profit Organization. We are engaged in transforming lives of children and enhance socio-ecnomic and literacy level.
What we do
We believe that a brighter future starts with education. So, We make sure to keep children in schools and provide a proper education

To provide proper Education to children we offer free tutoring center's in rural and urban areas. We do campaigning to increase the awreness in parents, to understand the importance of education.

Need

According to Global Campaign for Education Report, 2011:

★ 47% of the children drop out before 8th grade in suburbs/villages.
★ Drop out due to Poor Performance (PP) at school
★ Poor Performance (PP) is due to the illiteracy of the parents.
★ Parents are not able to help children with schoolwork at home.
★ TUTORING CENTERS can fulfill this need and keep the children at school.

services

Kingdom Planters engage the children after school hours and provide free tutoring services to children in villages and suburbs.

Rent Halls
